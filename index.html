The Strokes

The Strokes es una banda de rock alternativo que se hizo muy popular en los años 2000. Su estilo es *simple pero muy pegajoso*, y muchas personas consideran que ayudaron a revivir el rock moderno. Con canciones como “Last Nite” y “Reptilia”, lograron convertirse en una de las bandas más importantes de su generación.

By bringing style, fun, and catchy songwriting back to rock music, the Strokes became one of the most influential bands of the early 21st century. Their direct, hooky sound -- equally inspired by classic tunesmiths like and the brash attitude of fellow New Yorkers and -- set trends even as it looked back on rock history. With their acclaimed 2001 debut, Is This It, the Strokes reinvigorated a rock scene that had spent years focused on post-grunge and nu-metal. Along with and , they helped shape the sound and look of alternative rock in the 2000s, with , , and , , and reflecting their impact in the U.S. and the U.K., respectively. As the decade unfolded, the Strokes embellished on their back-to-basics style, adding new wave and synth pop elements to 2003's Room on Fire and 2006's ambitious First Impressions of Earth, a trend they continued with later releases like 2013's Comedown Machine. Though they spent much of the 2010s working on individual projects, they returned with some of their most
passionate-sounding music on 2020's The New Abnormal.
