Fotos con su estilo

The Strokes redefined 2000s indie rock with their raw debut Is This It (2001), followed by the tighter Room on Fire (2003) and the more experimental First Impressions of Earth (2006). After a hiatus, they released the polished Angles (2011) and Comedown Machine (2013), later returning with the Grammy-winning The New Abnormal (2020)
